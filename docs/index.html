Independence & Affiliation Notice

The MCHS Sports Almanac is an independent historical and statistical archive. It is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated in coordination with Meade County High School, Meade County Schools, or any associated athletic department.

Project Scope

This archive prioritizes accuracy, repeatable structure, and long-term usability. Completed seasons are treated as historical artifacts, not living documents.
