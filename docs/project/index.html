Project Overview

Scope, principles, and archival methodology

Project Scope

The MCHS Sports Almanac is a long-term historical and statistical archive of Meade County High School athletics, maintained for reporting, research, and historical preservation purposes.

Recognition Principle

A long-term goal of this project is to recognize every athlete who has suited up for Meade County High School athletics, at any level, where records exist.

Not all athletes will have complete statistical records. In many cases, recognition may be limited to roster inclusion, season participation, or contemporaneous mention.

The absence of statistics should not be interpreted as the absence of participation.

Data Philosophy

This archive values participation and contribution, not just measurable outcomes. Statistical completeness varies by era, sport, and source.
